## Project Title
Mind My Way — AI-Powered Mental Health Journaling & Clinical Reporting

## What You Built
A mobile mental health app demonstrator with 4 interfaces: (1) patient profile with guardian consent for under-18s, (2) daily journaling with mood/energy/stress sliders, DSM symptom tracking, reflective journaling with audio transcription, and AI chat with PII abstraction, (3) patient dashboard with 2-week charts, AI-generated weekly reflections, energy boosters, stress triggers, and DSM-5 risk assessment with GP referral, (4) clinician view with AI thematic analysis, PHQ-9/ASRS questionnaire sender, condition checkboxes, clinical notes, and export. Uses Claude API for chat, thematic analysis, and reflection generation. 14 days of pre-seeded demo data.
